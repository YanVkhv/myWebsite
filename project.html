The idea

Have you ever wanted to sign up for a gym membership and get in shape?
I'm sure you have, and it probably went a little like this:
You're incredibly motivated the first few weeks, you hit the gym each day, slowly but surely you lose the drive and stop going entirely.
You signed up for a year and only end up going once or twice.
Or maybe you feel so intimidated by the fit girls and jacked dudes that you end up skipping gym sessions.
Wouldn't it be nice to have someone accompany you, motivate you and help you?
Not everyone can afford a 100€/hour trainer, so why not help each other?

How it'll actually work

With the myGymBuddy© app you can browse and swipe for friends with the same goals as you, meet up and hit the gym together!

Do you want to lose weight?
Find someone with the same weight goal, meet up and go to the gym together!

Do you want to increase your bench, squat or deadlift?
Find someone with the same PR's, motivate each other, spot each other and just go for that next all time high together!

Are you a scrawny kid looking to change your life for the better but are too intimidated to hit the weights?
Find someone with the same struggles and go together!

With the myGymBuddy© app you will be able to track the progress of you and your friends, start chatting, share workouts, but most importantly, make new friends!
